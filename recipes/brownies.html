Brownies

Description

A simple brownie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 dl flour
  • 100 g chocolate
  • 100 g butter
  • 2 dl sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ts vanilla extract
  • 100 g butter
  • 1 ts baking powder
  1. Mix melted chocolate and butter with sugar.
  2. Add eggs.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients together.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the mixture.
  5. Pour into a baking tin.
  6. Bake at 175 degrees celsius for 20 minutes.
