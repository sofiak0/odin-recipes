Muffins

Description

A simple muffin recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 dl flour
  • 1 dl butter
  • 2 dl sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 dl milk
  • 1 dl cocoa powder
  • 1 ts baking powder
  • 1 ts baking soda
  • 100 g chocolate
  1. Whisk sugar and eggs.
  2. Add melted butter and milk.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients together.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the mixture.
  5. Mix the chocolate into the dough.
  6. Divide the dough into a muffin tin.
  7. Bake at 200 degrees celsius for 20 minutes.
