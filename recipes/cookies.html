Cookies

Description

A simple cookie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 5 dl flour
  • 200 g butter
  • 2 dl sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ts vanilla extract
  • 100g butter
  • 1 ts baking powder
  • 1 ts baking soda
  • 3 dl chocolate chips
  1. Mix sugar and melted butter together.
  2. Add the egg and whisk.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients together.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the mixture.
  5. Mix the chocolate chips into the dough.
  6. Shape the dough into small balls on a baking tray.
  7. Bake at 200 degrees celsius for 10 minutes.
